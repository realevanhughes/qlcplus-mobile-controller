# Google Play privacy policy

## What data is collected?
This app runs entirely on-device with the only network connection being the one to your chosen QLC+ host.

The only data collected by me (Evan Hughes, https://unit.link/evanh) is via the Google Play dashboard.
This means I can only see basic download statistics such as reigon and device type.
I have no control over the collection of this data because it is mandatory for Google Play.
I do not recieve ongoing analytics, only the initial download from Google Play and if you were to uninstall.

This app does not use Google firebase or any other analytics service for the Google Play build, APK or otherwise.

If any of these Google Play analytics concern you, feel free to download the latest release in APK form from this repository (or build from source?!).
